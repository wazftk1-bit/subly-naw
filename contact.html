Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How does the free trial work?

Start with a 14-day free trial of our Professional plan. No credit card required. You'll get full access to all features including AI analysis and unlimited subscription tracking.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any difference in billing.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption. We only read subscription metadata — never your actual emails, files, or content. Your data is never sold or shared.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Yes, our Business plan includes custom integrations, dedicated support, and team management. For larger organizations, contact us for a tailored solution.

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with Gmail, QuickBooks, Stripe, Slack, and most major SaaS platforms. New integrations are added regularly based on customer feedback.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. No hidden fees, no questions asked. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.
