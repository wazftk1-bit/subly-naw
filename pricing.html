Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our plans

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any difference.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Professional plan includes a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. Business plan includes a 30-day trial.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade instantly or archive old subscriptions to stay within your plan.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund.

Can I pay yearly?

Yes! Annual billing saves you 20%. You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for Business plans.

Need a Custom Solution?

For organizations with 100+ employees or unique requirements, we offer custom enterprise packages with dedicated support, SLA guarantees, and tailored integrations.
